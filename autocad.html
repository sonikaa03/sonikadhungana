Project Overview

This project includes detailed AutoCAD drawings of floor plans, sections, and elevations. The floor plans illustrate room layouts and flow between spaces, sections show interior heights and structural details, and elevations provide a view of the building's exterior from multiple angles. Together, these drawings give a clear picture of the overall design and layout.
